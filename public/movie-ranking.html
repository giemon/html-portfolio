My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Last Samurai

A movie about true virtue. I love the spirit and the atmosphere of the Samurais' village.

Kung Fu Panda

A very deep yet fun and super beautiful anime movie of all time.

Forrest Gump

Deeply moving movie about the person with huge heart bravely facing the suffering and pains of life.
